Welcome to my car application

This application is a simple application that allows you to view cars. You can view the list of cars, view the details of a car and add a car to the list.

How to use the application?

To use the application, you just have to click on the "Cars" tab. You will then be able to view the list of cars. You can also click on a car to view its details.

If you want to add a car to the list, you just have to click on the "Add" button. You will then be able to add a car to the list.
